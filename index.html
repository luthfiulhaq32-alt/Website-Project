How It Works

How We Handle a Project

Here's what happens after you contact us — from first conversation to delivery.

01
You Tell Us What You Need

Get in touch by WhatsApp, email, or a call. Tell us what you have in mind — the space, the size, any reference images you've seen. It doesn't have to be a fully formed brief. We'll ask the right questions. No charge for the conversation.

02
We Put Together a Proposal

Based on what you've told us, we suggest a direction — size, finish, pattern — and pair the project with the right craftsman. You get a written quote with materials, timeline, and price. No surprises later.

03
You Approve a Sample First

Before we make the full order, we produce a sample piece so you can check the finish and material in person. If something needs adjusting, we do it before production starts.

04
Production With Updates

We keep an eye on the production and send you photos at key stages — the base shape, the finish, and before packing. You're not left wondering what's happening.

05
We Check It Before It Ships

Before anything leaves the workshop, we check the finish, dimensions, and build quality. If something isn't right, it gets fixed before it reaches you.

06
Delivery & Care Guide

We arrange delivery to your address. Every order comes with a short guide on how to clean and maintain the copper. For large installations, we can coordinate with whoever is doing the fitting.

About Us

What We Do and Where We're From

Tumang Copper Works is based around Tumang — a village in Boyolali, Central Java, that has been known for copper craft for a long time.

The craftsmen there learned from their families. It's not a recent industry — people in Tumang have been doing this work for generations, and it shows in how they approach a piece.

We work between those craftsmen and clients who need custom copper pieces — hotels, designers, architects. We manage the project so both sides can do what they're good at.

You get well-made copper work and a clear, organised process. We're not a factory and we're not a marketplace — we're the person who makes sure your project gets done properly.

The Craftsmen We Work With

We work with workshops in Tumang, Boyolali. Before we send any project to a workshop, we've checked their work in person — quality, consistency, and whether they deliver on time.

We Check the Work

Every piece is checked before it ships — finish, size, and build. We use imported copper and brass with consistent thickness. If something's wrong, we deal with it before it reaches you.

We Manage the Process

One person handles your project from start to finish — the quote, samples, production updates, and delivery. You don't need to chase multiple people.

You Can Visit the Workshop

If you'd like to see the workshop before committing to an order, that's fine. We can arrange a visit to Boyolali. Just let us know in advance.
